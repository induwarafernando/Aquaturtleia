KOSGODA TURTLE HATCHERY

Welcome to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a conservation center located in Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka. Its primary focus is on the preservation and protection of sea turtles. The hatchery serves as a sanctuary where sea turtle eggs are incubated, and hatchlings are cared for until they can be released into the ocean. The hatchery aims to raise awareness about sea turtle conservation and offers visitors a unique opportunity to witness the nesting and hatching process. By visiting the hatchery, individuals can actively contribute to the survival of these magnificent creatures. Releasing baby turtles into the ocean is an unforgettable experience that allows visitors to make a positive impact on sea turtle populations.
Whether you are a nature enthusiast, a curious traveler, or someone looking to contribute to conservation efforts, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery welcomes you to explore its facilities and learn about the wonders of sea turtles. By protecting and preserving these ancient mariners, we ensure their continued presence in our oceans for generations to come.

The hatchery provides a remarkable backdrop for its vital conservation efforts. Visitors can witness the captivating sight of sea turtles gracefully navigating their natural habitat as they explore the sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Hikkaduwa has become a cherished home for these ancient mariners, attracting visitors from around the globe who are captivated by their majestic presence.
The hatchery itself exemplifies the delicate harmony between nature and human intervention. Its strategic positioning safeguards sea turtle nests from potential threats, creating a safe haven for the incubation of their precious eggs. Through vigilant monitoring and protection of nesting sites, the hatchery plays a vital role in preserving these endangered species.
Immerse yourself in the wonders of the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, where you can witness the magic of sea turtles' nesting and hatching processes while surrounded by the serene beauty of Hikkaduwa. By supporting the hatchery's efforts, you contribute to the conservation and survival of these incredible creatures, ensuring that they continue to grace the oceans for generations to come.
